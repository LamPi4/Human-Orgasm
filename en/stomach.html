Stomach


Ấn để chuyển qua tiếng Việt
  • The stomach is a pouch-like structure, capable of holding a maximum volume of about 3 liters. It stores and processes food.
  • The stomach wall consists of four main layers:
  • the serosa
    1. The muscularis externa notably thick and strong, composed of three layers: :
      1. Longitudinal muscle
      2. Circular muscle
      3. Diagonal muscles
    2. The submucosa
    3. The mucosa (stomach lining) contains numerous gastric glands that secrete gastric juices.
