Liver


Ấn để chuyển qua tiếng Việt
  • The small intestine is very long (about 2 to 3 meters) and narrow in diameter.
    It transforms food into simple substances that the body can absorb.
  • Its wall consists of 4 layers: the serosa, muscularis externa, submucosa and mucosa.
  • The small intestine has a dense network of blood
    capillaries and lymphatic capillaries distributed throughout the villi to facilitate nutrient absorption.
