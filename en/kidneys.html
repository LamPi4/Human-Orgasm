Kidneys

Ấn để chuyển qua tiếng Việt
  • Usually, each person has two kidneys.
    • The basic functional unit of kidneys is the nephron:
    1. Proximal convoluted tubule
    2. Henle's loop
    3. Distal convoluted tubule
    4. The Collecting duct
  • The kidney is made up of two main parts
    1. Renal Cortex Region
      • Renal corpuscle
      • Bowman's capsule
      • Renal columns
      • Renal parenchyma
    2. Renal Medulla Region
      • Renal pelvis
      • Renal papilla
      • Renal pyramids
      • About 1 million nephron
  • The primary function of the kidneys is to filter the blood to remove waste products, maintain water and electrolyte balance, and regulate blood pressure and nutrient concentrations in the body.
