Lungs

Ấn để chuyển qua tiếng Việt
  • Surrounding the lungs are 2 layers of membranes: the parietal pleura, tightly adheres to the chest walls, and the visceral
    pleura tightly covers the lungs.
  • Between them is pleura fluid, a thin layer of fluid that creates negative intrapleural pressure, allowing the lungs to expand
    and become spongy.
  • The lungs contain 700 to 800 million alveoli, which provide a surface area for gaseous exchange of up to 70 to 80m2.
  • The function involves gas exchange between the external environment and the blood within the pulmonary circulation.
